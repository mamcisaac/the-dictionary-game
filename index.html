      How to Play

      Quick Guide

      1

      Start — Click New Game to see the word's first letter and one definition.

      2

      Guess — Enter any word beginning with that letter and press Enter.

      3

      Need a hint? — Click "Get a Clue" to see available clue types and their costs (2-7 points).

      4

      Clue types — Definitions, word length, examples, synonyms/antonyms, and letter reveals.

      5

      Scoring — You begin with 100 points. Clues cost 2-7 points each. After the first free guess, each extra guess costs 3 points.
