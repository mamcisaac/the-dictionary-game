      🎮 How to Play

      Welcome to The Dictionary Game! Guess mystery words using definitions and clues. Can you solve them all?

      🎯 Basic Gameplay

      1

      Start Playing

      Click "New Game" or "Daily Challenge" to begin. You'll see the first letter and primary definition of a mystery word.

      2

      Make Your Guess

      Type your guess and press Enter or click "Guess". Your first guess is always FREE!

      3

      Get More Clues

      If you're wrong, click "Get Clue" for additional hints. Each clue costs 5 points.

      📋 Rules & Scoring

      💯

      Starting Score

      You start with 100 points

      🆓

      First Guess

      Your first guess is always free

      💰

      Additional Guesses

      Cost: 1 + clues used points

      💡

      Clues

      Each clue costs 5 points (9 total available)

      ✅

      Valid Words

      Must be real English words starting with the given letter

      🎯

      Goal

      Guess correctly with the highest score possible

      🔍 Clue Types

      FREE First Letter + Primary Definition
      -5pts Alternative Definitions (up to 3)
      -5pts Usage Examples (up to 2)
      -5pts Letter Reveals (up to 3 more letters)
