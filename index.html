      How to Play

      1. Start the Game

      Click "New Game" to begin. You'll see the first letter and definition of a mystery word.

      2. Make Your Guess

      Type your guess in the input field and press Enter or click "Guess".

      3. Valid Guesses

      Your guess must: 1) Start with the given letter, and 2) Be a real English word. Invalid guesses are rejected without penalty.

      4. Get Hints

      If you're wrong, you can ask for more clues. You get 7 additional clues (2 are free at start). Each clue costs 5 points.

      5. Clue Structure

      Every word provides 9 total clues: First definition and letter (free), then up to 3 more definitions, 2 examples, and 3 letter reveals.

      6. Scoring

      Start with 100 points. Each clue costs 5 points (minimum score: 1). Wrong guesses cost 1 + number of clues used. Smart guessing early saves points!

      7. Strategy

      Clues alone can't end the game (min 1 point), but wrong guesses can! Early guesses are cheaper than late ones.

      5. Track Progress

      Check your statistics to see your win rate and best scores.
